My Daily Life

This section contains information about my daily life. My daily life starts with a ringing alarm at around 6:00 AM in the morning. After waking up, I get ready and dressed up for meal. Then I have breakfast at around 7:00 AM. After having breakfast, I go to school at around 8:00 AM. I come back from school at around 3:00 PM. After coming back from school, I do my homework and assessment at around 4:00 PM. After doing homework and assessment, I have free time to do whatever I want to do. I usually spend my free time by playing games, watching movies and listening to music. After spending some time in free time, I have dinner at around 8:00 PM. After having dinner, I get ready for bed and sleep at around 10:00 PM. This is how my daily life goes on.
